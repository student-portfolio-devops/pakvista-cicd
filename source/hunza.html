Hunza Valley

Discover Hunza Valley with Rakaposhi viewpoint, Attabad Lake, and Baltit Fort.

Rakaposhi Viewpoint

Rakaposhi is one of the most spectacular peaks in the Karakoram range. The viewpoint near Hunza offers dramatic panoramas of the mountain's snowy summit, steep glaciers, and surrounding valleys. Visitors can enjoy sunrise and sunset views that illuminate the peak in golden light.

Attabad Lake

Attabad Lake was formed after a massive landslide in 2010. The lake's deep turquoise water contrasts with the rugged mountains around it, creating a striking natural landscape. Boat rides across Attabad Lake are a popular way to experience the calm water and steep blue cliffs.

Baltit Fort

Baltit Fort is an ancient fortress above Karimabad, built over 700 years ago. The fort has been restored and opened to visitors, showcasing Hunza's cultural history, architecture, and traditional lifestyle. From the fort, travelers can look out over terraced fields, fruit orchards, and the valley below.
